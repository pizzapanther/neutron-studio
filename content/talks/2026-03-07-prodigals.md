## Who are the Prodigals?

## The Weight of Guilt

- Even God has prodigals
  - story of Adam and Eve, Genesis 2 & 3
  - story of Jonah and the Whale
  - kings of Israel
- dedication of our children to God
  - 1 Samuel 1
- [Proverbs 22:6](https://biblehub.com/proverbs/22-6.htm), a principal, not a guarantee

## Some Prodigals We Have Made

- Do we judge too harshly or unfairly
  - [Matthew 7:3-5](https://biblehub.com/nlt/matthew/7.htm)
  - [1 Corinthians 10:23](https://biblehub.com/1_corinthians/10-23.htm)
- Are you ashamed of your kids?
- What is their heart?
  - are they really that far from God?

## Have Love of the Father

- Have you forgiven your prodigals?
  - [Luke 17:4](https://biblehub.com/luke/17-4.htm)
- The church and our households must have the love of the Father
